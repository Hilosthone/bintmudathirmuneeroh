To Doc. Bint Muneeroh — The Heart That Guides My Words

Some people teach with books in hand,

But you, dear Doc, you helped me stand.

Not just in rhythm, rhyme, or meter,

But as a writer, and something deeper.

You saw the trembling in my voice,

Yet still, you smiled and gave me choice —

To trust my pen, to shape my name,

To find my truth inside the flame.

You never asked for thanks or praise,

You simply gave, in countless ways.

You met each line I wrote with care,

And shaped my thoughts with love and flair.

In every poem I now create,

Your kindness echoes through each gate.

You've been the calm, the light, the guide,

Who stood so gently by my side.

So here's to you on this special day,

With heartfelt words I long to say:

May joy surround you, soft and wide,

Like poems blooming deep inside.

And may you know, both near and far,

How treasured, loved, and rare you are.

Happy Birthday, my mentor,

This poet walks, because of you.

— Hilosthone ✍️
